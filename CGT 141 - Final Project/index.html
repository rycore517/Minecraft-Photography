Brief About Me

Hello my name is Ryan Corey and I am a first year UX Designer at Purdue.

Before UX Design, I was in Game Development where I spent my time learning how to develop experience for gamers. That's where I believe my interest for designing came from however I didn't just want to design for a specific audience I wanted more. So I made the decision to transfer into UX and I have learned and gained experience for many things, and I am looking forward to more in the future.
